Other Important Things to Keep in Mind While Doing the Homework

  • Late homeworks are accepted.
  • Make sure to show all your work. Getting the correct answer even without work will not get marks.
  • When solving Math problems, always end with a concluding sentence. It must have this "therefore" symbol: "∴" and then followed by a meaningful conclusion sentence. For example, "∴ The answer is three." is not meaningful if the question asks "How many apples does Alice have?". The better answer is: "∴ Alice has three apples."
  • Do not forget to write your units in your final answer. Also, it must be the correct units. For example, if a square's side length is 6 metres, then its area is 36 sq. metres and not just 36 metres.
  • Box your final answer so I know what your answer is.
  • Proper math format is required.
