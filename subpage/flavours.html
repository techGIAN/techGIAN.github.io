A Food Journal


I have recently begun exploring the kitchen world and what better way than to do this than documenting my fandom for food and my experience wearing the apron and chef hat. I haven't cooked/baked that much, but anything that I am proud of (or perhaps even those that flop), will get a spotlight in my page. I will include a couple of the pictures I took while preparing my dishes, any motivations I may have that led me to prepare the meal, the recipe I used (of course I am not a masterchef so I do not own any recipe, thus I got them online and will have to cite that of course), and the final verdict presenting the results of my creation! Ready? Chow!

About

Flavours is a food journal webpage created by Gian Alix talking about his motivations, experiences and constructive criticisms on food and dishes he has prepared. Not a professional chef, but rather an aspiring wanna-be to showcase and share his passions of food. This is his diary documenting his encounters and hands-on on food. Got something in mind you'd like to suggest for him to create as a masterpiece? Let us know here!
