  • May 03, 2020: I wrote a quick write-up today on Adaptive Simpson's Rule, which was supposedly an extension to the Simpson's Rule write-up from yesterday. It is basically a recursive algorithm that takes into account the error produced by Simpson's Rule on smaller and smaller intervals of the bigger interval while it does not continue to satisfy the stopping condition, which is actually specified by threshold error.
