My Feelings Towards Conspiracy Theories

IN MY HEAD   |   Date Posted: December 28, 2020

Photo by Simba Munemo on Unsplash

So far, I have already written 3 write-ups of Conspiracy Theories (I plan on making this a series). I still have yet to write my fourth, which I plan on writing and releasing sometime in late December. My future plans on this series will continue towards 2021 but I cannot make promises as I have lots of work and deadlines.

Anyhow today (this is not Part 4 by the way), I would like to express my deep feelings and concerns for writing conspiracy theories. Although I have been doing so in the past, I have always kept on writing disclaimers notifying that I don’t intend to harm anyone so please don’t sue. And that I am merely expressing my thoughts and opinions on the matter. It’s only fun and entertainment and shouldn’t be taken too seriously and should only present itself to be as food for the brain or thought-provoking if you will. There are theories out there or rumors that people have gone missing, abducted or even killed after talking about conspiracy theories as a way to silence them. It is kinda scary that I am touching these waters when I am not an expert in the subject matter. Sometimes, my theories are even so silly that they just don’t have credibility whatsoever. But I talk about it. Why? To express myself. To talk about things. Honestly, I am a really sad, lonely, depressed person who doesn’t really have friends (and also no siblings). So instead of feeling sorry for myself and feeling all sad, I repress it by keeping myself busy - doing so many homeworks, assignments, projects, writings, works, research, etc. But back to the theories, I just have no one to talk to about these things. These are some things that seem like we can think about them.

But the scary part is that the fact that people have gone missing or even found dead just by being getting too involved maybe? There are theories out there too about how our phones are listening to what we say and even knows our activities and what we do. Could these info be used to help us be eradicated by anyone who wants us out? It may also be possible that there are current confidential top-secret research workings where they are developing some AI that can be installed our phones that determines a person’s thoughts and feelings. And so even if you are not saying anything and just thinking about it, then you are still vulnerable and that you can easily be silenced before even making a move?

These should have been in one part of the Conspiracy Theories series. But I decided to just take it out due to the fact that I would like to talk more about my inner feelings and thoughts and emotions on it and not mixing it with the actual theories. A sepration of concerns if you will. I will end it here, but my next post or two will likely be about the fourth part of the Conspiracy Theories.
