Dystopian Love

FICTION   |   Date Posted: September 06, 2020

Photo by Eric Ward on Unsplash

This is a cute little love story that I wrote 6 years ago, when I was still in my old university (probably I was 18 years old). I had some time off from school, and got inspired or motivated somehow. Seeing that it's that long ago, there may be some grammar issues and spelling errors and some cringy stuff here and there, plus some words and terms that are only from that university, but I'm pretty sure you can catch along regardless. (Note the paragraphs are numbered, as I remember that I wrote a short analysis on it with the numbers used to help). But anyways, the story is about a troublemaker girl (around her teenage years) named Sheena, who have already been subjected by the discipline head into several punsihments such as comunity service. On her last straw, the head decided to call forth his best student, in academics, leadership and community-related activities, Silpher, who's a very much nice guy and avoids any conflicts (well, he has some inner-bad boy personality that he doesn't show easily). The discipline head decides to subject Sheena into a punishment where she will be handcuffed to Silpher. The question is, will this turn nasty or will opposites attract?
